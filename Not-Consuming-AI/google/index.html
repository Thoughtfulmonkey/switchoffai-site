Google search

AI summaries might appear on the search results page.

Using the -AI search term

The easiest way to both remove AI summaries on the search results page and AI results is as follows:

  • Include -AI in your search.

For example, country with the longest border -AI.

Filtering to websites only

By filtering the search results to just websites, you can remove the AI summaries. This also removes other widgets, like People also ask, as well as video and image suggestions. To apply the filter, do the following:

  • Add &udm=14 to the end of the address after you perform a search. For example, https://www.google.com/search?q=country+with+the+longest+border&udm=14.

To make search more convenient, you can use udm14.org. If you perform a search there, it redirects you to a Google search with the udm filter applied.
