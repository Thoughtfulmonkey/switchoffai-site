Uninstall Copilot

Copilot is the AI assistant from Microsoft. It might be preinstalled on your computer or be added as part of an update. To uninstall copilot, complete the following steps:

  1. In the search box on the taskbar, enter add or remove, and then select Add or remove programs in the search results.
  2. On the Installed apps page, choose one of the following options:
    • In the Search apps box, enter copilot.
    • Scroll down the list until you see the Copilot app.
  3. Select More options (ellipsis icon) for the Copilot app, and then select Uninstall.
  4. Follow any additional instructions to uninstall Copilot.
