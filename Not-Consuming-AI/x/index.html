X (aka Twitter)

Grok is the AI service from X, which famously referred to itself as MechHitler. To mute Grok so that you don't see its posts, complete the following steps:

  1. Go to https://x.com/grok.
  2. Select the three dots (elipses) button, and then select Mute from the menu that appears.
