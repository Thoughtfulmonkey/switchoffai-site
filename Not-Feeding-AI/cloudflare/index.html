Cloudflare

Cloudflare provide services that sit between your website and the people that access your website. This includes a content delivery network (CDN), which uses geographically distributed copies of your website to provide users with faster access, and distributed denial of service (DDoS) attack mitigation.

Like the majority of modern tech companies, Cloudflare is heavily invested in providing AI services. However, it can also help you to protect your site from AI.

Blocking AI crawlers

Many generative AI services scan sites on the internet and use the content as training data. If you use Cloudflare and want to stop your site from being used to train AI, refer to the instructions in the Cloudflare documentation: Block AI Bots
